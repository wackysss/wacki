The culture of the Philippines is a blend of indigenous Austronesian and Hispanic influences. The country has a rich cultural heritage, including traditional music, dance, and art. The Philippines is also known for its vibrant festivals, such as the Feast of the Black Nazarene and the Sinulog Festival. The country is predominantly Catholic, with over 80% of the population practicing the religion.
