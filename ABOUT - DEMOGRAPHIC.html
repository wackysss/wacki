As of 2022, the population of the Philippines is estimated to be around 108 million people. The country is home to a diverse mix of ethnic groups, including Tagalog, Cebuano, Ilocano, and Bicolano. The official language of the Philippines is Filipino, which is based on the Tagalog language. English is also widely spoken in the country.
